Privacy
-------

Heart Observe stores heart pressure, pulse and related data that you provide. This
data is stored only on your device.
It does not
associate any other personal identifiable information with the heart data
other that what you could include in the *notes* field.
All the heart data is visible to you through the app and you may delete any or
all the information whenever you like. If you uninstall the application,
all the heart data is removed as well.

To help protect your privay, no advertising is used in Heart Observe.
Please support this policy by purchasing the Heart Observe
(donate) app. This allows for ongoing development of this app and others like
it.
