Privacy Policy

Heart Observe stores heart pressure, pulse and related data that you provide. This data is stored only on your device. It does not associate any other personal identifiable information with the heart data other than what you may include in the notes field. All of this data is visible to you through the app and you may delete any or all of the information whenever you like. If you uninstall the application, all of the data is removed as well.

To help protect your privacy, no advertising is used in Heart Observe. Please support this policy by purchasing the Heart Observe (Donate) app (see home page for details). Your donation allows for ongoing development of this and similar apps.
